PC Monitor & Control

A native iOS & Android app for monitoring and controlling your Windows PC over local WiFi — no cloud, no subscriptions, fully private.

📱 iOS & Android
🪟 Windows
📶 Local WiFi
🔒 No Cloud
Features
📊
Real-time Hardware Monitoring
CPU, GPU, RAM, fans, temperatures, disks, and network stats — updated live every 2 seconds.
🎛️
Fully Customisable Dashboard
Drag to reorder cards, rename them, pick icons, and add custom sensor cards for any metric.
🖥️
Multiple PC Support
Monitor and switch between multiple PCs on your local network from one app.
🔌
PC Controls
Shutdown, restart, sleep, and lock your PC directly from your phone.
🔒
Completely Private
No accounts, no cloud, no telemetry. Everything stays on your local network between your phone and your PC.
🎮
Demo Mode
Try the full app experience without a PC — explore all cards with live simulated data.
